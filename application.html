APPLICATION

This is an introduction to the product application of Shenzhen ANLANHECHUANG Tech Co.,Ltd

BACKLIT APPLICATION

UV backlit has been widely used in domestic and overseas sign market, such as APPLE , HUAWEI, OPPO smart phone 4S shops, subway advertisement, airport advertisement etc. as well as Supermarket signs and advertisement. ALHC focuses on developing advanced UV roll to roll printer to offer the best solution for backlit application.

Media

Soft film
Soft film media price is competitive, thin, bright white, good visual effect. It’s widely used on the ceilings, Can be used for hotels, bars, meeting rooms, supermarket etc. Like below pictures.
Attention:
1.Softfilm is very sensitive for the heat, need LED lamp to cure the ink. Otherwise the materials will be distorted.
2.If the materials too thin, it will be very difficult to print good. And also difficult for ink adhesion.
3.Pay attention when you load the materials.
4.Need additional soft film feeding module when you print this materials.

Backlit fabric

High end advertisement light box, especially for IPhone, HUAWEI, OPPO smart phone shops.
Attention:
Need very good and stable flexible uv ink.

Backlit film

Usually using PET film ( both flexible and rigid one) , good stiffness, fine print texture, high and low temperature resistant, waterproof and anti UV color fading. The width can reach 3.2 meters, which is suitable for the big indoor and outdoor advertisement. The printed colors very bright, fine and smooth.
Attention:
There have a lots of static during the printing, suggest client to add anti-static bar on the machine.

Backlit banner

Materials thick, high quality display effect, high intensity, double sunscreen and double wind resistance.
Application: Big outdoor advertisement, banners.
The feature of banner:
1.There are 2 kinds banner, shiny and matt, shiny usually for indoor, matt for both indoor and outdoor use.
2. high strength, good ductility, flexibility. This unique nature makes this kind of material widely used in large scale, high-end supermarket.
3. Smooth surface, strong ink absorption. The chemical properties of the materials determine that they have strong ink absorbability and can be attached to high strength under the conditions of oily or water-based ink.
4. The physical and chemical properties are stable, mainly in oxidation resistance, acid resistance and alkali resistance and cold tolerance.
